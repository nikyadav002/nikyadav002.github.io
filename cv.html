About

I'm a PhD student at IIT Delhi working in computational materials science, using first-principles methods, molecular dynamics, and machine learning to study soft semiconductors at the atomic level. My research broadly examines how structure shapes carrier dynamics, defects, and excited-state processes, with a wider interest in data-driven materials discovery.
